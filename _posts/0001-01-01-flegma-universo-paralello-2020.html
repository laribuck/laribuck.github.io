TesseracTstudio is one of the worldwide leading psytrance & progressive trance music labels from Serbia.

Launching on 2010, TesseracTstudio have become home major Serbian projects such as Zyce, Talpa, Flegma, E-Clip, Ectima, Sideform, Relativ, Vertex, Arhetip, Nerso, Sonic Entity, Manmachine and many more. It is also recognized as creator of #serbianstyle psy-progressive.

Our artists have performed at most of the major psytrance festivals such as: BOOM, Ozora, Universo Paralelo, Rainbow Serpent, Psy Fi, Psychedelic Experience, Indian Spirit, One Love, Psychedelic Circus, Mandallah, S.U.N. & many more.
