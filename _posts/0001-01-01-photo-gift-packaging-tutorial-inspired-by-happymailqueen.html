Here’s a fun way to gift your kids’ school photos for the holidays! The recipients most likely have frames from previous years...so all they need are updated pictures. Present the new prints in this pretty packaging.

Inspired by Happymailqueen: https://youtu.be/cHxC9Y-2VZ8

Supplies needed:
12” x 12” sheet of double-sided paper
Scrap paper for circles
Eyelets
Twine
Double-sided tape
Sewing machine (optional)
Paper trimmer
Score board
Bone folder
Cropadile
Corner rounder
Scissors
