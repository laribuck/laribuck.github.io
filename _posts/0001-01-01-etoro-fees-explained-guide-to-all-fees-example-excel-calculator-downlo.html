I'll cover following topics on Etoro:

1. Withdrawal fee
As there is $25 withdrawal fee.

2. Currency Conversion Fee
Etoro operates with dollars only, so when you deposit money from other currencies, they take a conversion fee.

Just to confuse all the newbies, conversion fee is calculate in pips.
50pips for UK
250 pips for EUR

3. Spread Fee
Spread fees are charged when you buy anything on eToro, stocks, crypto currencies like bitcoin, and so on, however they are included in the buying price that you see in eToro.

Spread fee for stocks is 0.09%.

4. Overnight Fee
eToro charges also overnight fees, which is basically a small fee for them to lend you the money to hold the item overnight.

It doesn't always get applied, mostly when you borrow money (leverage) from etoro.
