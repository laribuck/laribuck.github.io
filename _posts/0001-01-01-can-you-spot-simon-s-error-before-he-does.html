Rules:
Normal Sudoku rules apply. In each row/column/9-cell irregular region, two digits must be shaded as "stars". These shaded cells cannot touch each other, even diagonally. Purple clues give the sum of the digits in the two shaded cells in their row/column. Grey clues give the sum of the digits sandwiched between the stars in their row/column.
