With this new change, when you hit the Retweet button on someone else’s tweet, instead of seeing a menu pop up that asks whether you want to retweet or quote tweet, you’ll simply be prompted to quote tweet. This is because Twitter is trying to promote users actually adding something to the topic rather than blindly retweeting information. If you have something to add, you can type in your comment here before retweeting. However, if you leave the text box empty and tweet it as is, it will be posted as a regular retweet. Though this makes the process more complicated, it’s still possible to retweet on Twitter without adding your own comment
