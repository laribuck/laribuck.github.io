It is a special state where the artist overcomes technical difficulties without extra effort to fully indulge in the joy of music making.

At Haydn's concerts, barriers were set up to control the frantic crowd.

But live instruments have fallen into the background by the 21st century.

At the same time, there are three artists who feel a burning desire to prove that classical music has not lost anything from its power and that classical instruments have a place in today's mainstream musical world.

This is the Rise of the Instruments.
