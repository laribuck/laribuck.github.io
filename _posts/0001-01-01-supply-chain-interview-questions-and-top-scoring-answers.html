QUALITIES REQUIRED TO WORK WITHIN SUPPLY CHAIN
- Build positive, long-lasting relationships with key suppliers, manufacturers and logistic companies;
- Have an understanding of the supply chain process;
Demonstrate a determined, positive and resolute attitude towards your duties;
- Ensure you have a thorough understanding of how the supply chain can impact both positively and negatively on an organization’s operations;
- Demonstrate accuracy skills, problem-solving skills and an ability to solve problems under pressure.

SUPPLY CHAIN INTERVIEW QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS
Q. Tell me about yourself and why we should hire you in this Supply Chain position?
Q. What is the ‘supply chain’?
Q. Why do you want to work in supply chain?
Q. Why have you chosen our company to work for in this supply chain position?
Q. Tell me one thing you like the most about supply chain, and one thing you dislike?
Q. Describe a time when you disagreed with your manager or supervisor?
