Another day, another Hollywood couple that bites the dust this week, making this the end of the road for the beloved Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom.
Just when we thought we’d catch a break in 2017, our ‘Teenage Dreams’ have officially been crushed to a million pieces…

If romantic getaways, naked paddle boarding and surprise birthday parties couldn’t keep Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom together, then it looks like the rest of us can say goodbye to even the slightest chance of finding true love… the couple, who had been dating for over 10 months, called it quits yesterday after a statement was released from their reps, saying QUOTE, “Before rumors or falsifications get out of hand, we can confirm that Orlando and Katy are taking respectful, loving space at this time.”

After going public with their relationship at last year’s 2016 Golden Globes, the two have been spotted traveling the world together hand-in-hand, supporting each other during campaign season and partaking in a little public PDA action.

They even spent the holidays together last year as Orlando joined Katy’s family in the states for both Thanksgiving and Christmas, and more recently seen over the weekend posing for a photo at the Oscars Vanity Fair after-party, although they did NOT walk the carpet together, so we can assume they were just putting on a friendly show until they were ready to release their statement.

Many rumors have been surfacing as to why the couple split, one in particular, referencing a photo of Orlando chatting with a mystery woman, getting awfully close to her face last week during a pre-Oscars event, however, that could’ve been nothing but a friendly conversation.

The couple seems to be keeping reasons for the split private, rightfully so, but we can only hope these two are able to remain friends during this difficult time.
