Each week, Michigan Pharmacy Students have the opportunity to connect with prominent alumni and faculty.

This important episode was hosted by Dean Vicki Ellingrod and addresses topics related to sexual harassment in pharmacy. Dr. Rebecca Smith shares her personal experiences and advocacy work.

Dr. Rebecca Smith, originally from Little Rock, Arkansas, received her Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Arkansas in Fayetteville, Arkansas in 2013. She went on to receive her Doctor of Pharmacy degree from the University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences (UAMS) in Little Rock, Arkansas in 2017. She then completed a PGY1 pharmacy residency at Regional One Health in Memphis, Tennessee. After finishing her PGY1 in 2018, she began her Critical Care PGY2 pharmacy residency at University of Kentucky HealthCare in Lexington, Kentucky. Upon completion of her PGY2 in 2019, she accepted a position at UAMS working with the trauma, emergency general surgery, and neurocritical care services.
