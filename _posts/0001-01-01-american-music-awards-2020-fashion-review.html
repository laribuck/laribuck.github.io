--- title: "AMERICAN MUSIC AWARDS 2020 FASHION REVIEW" image: "https:\/\/i.ytimg.com\/vi\/Cu5UbTVNCps\/hqdefault.jpg" vid_id: "Cu5UbTVNCps" categories: "Education" tags: ["Doja Cat","Dua Lipa","Megan Thee Stallion"] date: "2020-11-25T21:55:31+03:00" vid_date: "2020-11-24T16:01:10Z" duration: "PT43M59S" viewcount: "72496" likeCount: "3265" dislikeCount: "39" channel: "HauteLeMode" --- The American Music Awards 2020 was a whirlwind of fashion choices. Jennifer Lopez chose to wear an off the runway Balmain two piece set, which many said was less than flattering. Megan Thee Stallion appeared in Area, the budding young New York brand, and I'm hoping their relationship continues in the future! Doja Cat wore two different Vivienne Westwood looks, and while I can't truthfully say I loved them, I think it's a relationship I'm willing to keep my eye on. Maluma wore Thom Browne and while separately their both HOT, together I don't know what to do with myself. David Dobrik needs to step up his game, and I'm hoping he'll channel some Eastern European aesthetics with Balenciaga in the future. Billie Eilish is beginning to bore me, and I hope that in her next era, she stops giving us Missy Elliott reproductions and pivots her style to something a bit more original. BTS went from somber to Dynamite in two different suiting styles, and I wasn't totally mad! Overall, I wanted more, but willing to take what I'm getting.
