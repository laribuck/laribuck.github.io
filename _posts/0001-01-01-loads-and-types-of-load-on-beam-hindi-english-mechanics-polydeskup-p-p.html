TYPES OF LOAD ON BEAM,
POINT LOAD,CONCENTRATED LOAD, U V L, U D L,
1. Point load or concentrated load- a load, which is assumed to be acting at single point on a beam, is called point load or concentrated load. Unit of point load is N, kN or Tonne
2. U.D.L. or Uniformly distributed load- U.D.L. is distributed uniformly over the entire length or part of length of beam. Unit of U.D.L. is kN/m or tonne/m.
3. U.V.L. or uniformly varying load- U.V.L. varies uniformly along the length of beam.
