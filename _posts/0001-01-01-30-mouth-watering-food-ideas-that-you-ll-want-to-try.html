Are you looking for ways to make your recipes even tastier? In this video, I show you simple ideas from the best chefs.

I show you how to make delicious flavored ice-cream using skittles. You simply separate them in different colors, and you mash them up in the blender. Then, you add the mixture in some vanilla ice-cream, and finally, you layer it in a container.

If you’re having a party with some of your friends over, simply pour some coca-cola with some gelatin. Then pour the mixture in an ice-tray and serve to your guests.

Another great ice-lollies idea is to mix some gummy bears with some sprite and then freeze them. Then serve them to your friends.

I show you the simplest cookie recipe using melted chocolate and flour.

You can also use melted chocolate to make chocolate noodle bases and fill them with whipped cream on top. Simply, fill a glass with cold water and melt some chocolate. Then, pour it over the cold water and voila.
