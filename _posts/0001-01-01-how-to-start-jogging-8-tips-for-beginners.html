How to Start Jogging - 8 Tips for Beginners

Jogging is a form of trotting or running at a slow or leisurely pace. The main intention is to increase physical fitness with less stress on the body than from faster running but more than walking, or to maintain a steady speed for longer periods of time.

Best Jogging Tips For Better Health And Fitness

1. Warm-up
2. Set A Goal
3. Wear Running Shoes
4. Loosen Up
5. Push Your Chest Out
6. Start Slowly
7. Land Softly
8. Use Breathing Techniques
9. Jog In Different Directions
10. Consider some entertainment.
11. Look For Landmarks
12. Time Your Steps
13. Train In Intervals
14. Practice Box Jumps
15. Train Downhill
16. Drink Water
17. Beat Yourself
18. Stretch
19. Buy comfortable, appropriate clothes.
20. Stay loose and relaxed.

Jogging timings

1. Jog in the morning.
2. Jog in the evening.
3. Jog on your lunch break.
4. Jog to work or school.
5. Have a backup plan!

Jogging Benefits


1. Improves cardiovascular health.
2. Helps burn calories and aids weight loss.
3. Improves lung health.
4. Boosts stamina and endurance.
5. Improves muscle flexibility.
6. Builds core strength.
7. Strengthens the muscles in your lower body.
8. Keeps stress and anxiety at bay.
9. Lowers depression.
10. Lowers the risk of certain types of cancer.
11. Fights diabetes.
