Being called to the married life when you don’t have a spouse makes you feel like you’re putting your life on hold, waiting for someone to come along so you can live out your vocation. Jackie offers five tips to remember if you’re having a hard time being single.

1. Everybody has an ache only God can satisfy.
2. Be healthy and holy now.
3. Remember your death. Remember life is short.
4. God doesn’t show you gold and give you silver.
5. Be patient and trust God.
