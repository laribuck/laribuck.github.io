WORKOUT:

1a. (Conventional or Sumo) Deadlift 4X8
Rest 1-2 min
1b. Deficit Reverse Lunges 4X8 ea. side

2a. Glute Bridge w/ 2 sec pause at top 4X10
Rest 1-2 min
2b. Deficit DB Sumo Squat 4X10

3. 1 Up 2 Down Lying Hamstring Curls 4X10 ea side
Rest 45 sec between sets

4a. BW Reverse Hypers 3X15
Rest 20 seconds
4a. BW Side Lying Straight Leg Hip Abductions 3X20 ea. side
