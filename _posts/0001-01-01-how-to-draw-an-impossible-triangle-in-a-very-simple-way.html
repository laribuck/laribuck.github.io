The Penrose Triangle is an illusion of a 3-D shape on a 2-D surface. It is an impossible shape in 3-D. Our mind tries to interpret it as a three-dimensional object in space, rather than interpreting it as a two-dimensional object drawn on the paper.

We tend to believe what we see, unless there is information to make us doubt our visual information.
