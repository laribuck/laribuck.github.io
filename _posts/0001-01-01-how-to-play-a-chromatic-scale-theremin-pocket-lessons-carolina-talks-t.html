The second Theremin Pocket Lesson is all about the Chromatic Scale. Did you know that there are actually 13 finger positions?
My 8-finger position technique counts with 5 extra positions to designate the half-step intervals in between every whole-step. You can use the these 13 positions to play a chromatic scale, minor scales, whole tone scales and modes. If you already learned the major scale, you may want to try your hand on some new scales!
