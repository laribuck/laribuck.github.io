A game of 'Maracana' featuring Africa's freestyle champion Titi Kone was played in front of large crowds in the capital Abidjan on Monday.

'Maracana' is the name given to a uniquely Ivorian brand of freestyle football.

The small and dusty six-a-side pitch demands players use technique and skill to overcome opponents.

Goals can only be scored in the box, although there are no goalkeepers.
