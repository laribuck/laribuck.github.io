Mordhau is an absolutely amazing game, easily one of the best games I've played in a long time! 10/10! Truly an enjoyable experience!


If you liked playing chivalry you'll love this game! MORDHAU is a medieval multiplayer slasher that you can play in both first & third person . Fight on a hectic battlefield with up to 64 players as a mercenary in a fictional, but realistic world, where you will get to experience intense and responsive, and very satisfying melee combat!

It's not just massive battles! It has everything from small-scale engagements to 64-player all-out war in modes such as Frontline and Battle Royale. It also has:
Cooperative & offline play where you can fight waves of enemies alongside your friends in the cooperative Horde mode, or practice your dueling skills offline against AI.

Free-form melee and ranged combat, just like in Mount and Blade! Gain complete control over your character and attacks and develop your own way of fighting!

Customize your character with face sculpting, weapon cosmetics and a wide variety of armor!
Huge arsenal of different weapons & equipment! Battle enemies with a greatsword, rain arrows from above, or even a PITCHFORK!

Believable fights! You can really feel the weight of the swings, the force behind every attack! A game where fights look believable, MORDHAU really strikes a balance between gameplay and realism, and most importantly: FUN!
