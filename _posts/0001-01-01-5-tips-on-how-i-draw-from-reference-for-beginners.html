In this video I share my top 5 tips on how I use a reference photo and draw accordingly.

Ideally, I like to use 5 steps as part of reference drawing workflow. Depending on my skill level for a certain subject, I will skip a few steps but if it's my first time drawing something, I will follow with the 5 steps outlined in this video.

This process can work for both traditional art and digital art.
