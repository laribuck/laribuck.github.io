Modular and versatile, the Mission Master family uses the Rheinmetall PATH autonomous kit (A-kit) to aid troops in a wide variety of operations. The Armed Reconnaissance A-UGV is equipped with both intel-gathering tech and Rheinmetall Fieldranger remote-controlled weapon system to gain tactical information and provide fire support when necessary, keeping a man in the loop at all times. The Mission Master – Armed Reconnaissance is designed to execute high-risk scouting missions and deliver real-time common operating picture without endangering soldiers.
