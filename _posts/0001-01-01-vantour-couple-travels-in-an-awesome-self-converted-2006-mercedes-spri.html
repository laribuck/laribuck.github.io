-- The Story:
Hi, we are Jess and Rob and we currently live in Prague, in the Czech Republic.
Before, we lived in Switzerland and we actually have met in Florida. We share the passion for travelling and
exploring new places and all together, we have visited 6 continents. Still, there is so much more we want to see
and this is why we are moving from backpacking to van life.

We have bought a 2006 Mercedes Sprinter van and converted it for about a year. Now, we are almost done
and only fine tuning the last details.It really starts feeling like a little home on wheels and the first weekend
and vacation trips we went for only proved us that this is what we want to do.

With the current situation, we had to change our original plans, but one way or the other, our new plan will
definitely include a lot of travelling and exploring.
